Web Security
Why is important web security?

Know how to Web security is important to maintain hackers and cyber-thieves away from gaining unauthorized access to personal, private and sensitive data.

Users who keep update of secure practices can effectively detect and evade potential cyberattacks, thus changing behaviours that could compromise their own security or that of others.

"Being a digital citizen therefore means learning how to protect access to our devices and handle with care anything that can threaten the privacy and security of ourselves or others." Digital Citizen Education
